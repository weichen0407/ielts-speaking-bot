# Vocabulary Suggestions

## Session: Food & Eating Habits

### Words That Can Be Optimized

| Your Word | Better Alternative | Why |
|-----------|-------------------|-----|
| like to | tend to, prefer to, am inclined to | More natural and varied for expressing preferences |
| eat healthy diet | maintain a balanced diet, follow a nutritious diet | More precise and idiomatic |
| cost you money | can be expensive, is costly, strains your budget | More natural phrasing; "cost you money" is redundant |
| not healthy | unhealthy, less nutritious, lacking in nutritional value | More concise and formal |
| you know | (omit or replace with a pause/filler like "well" or "I mean") | Overused filler; reducing it sounds more confident |

### Professional Vocabulary by Topic

#### Food & Eating Habits
- **nutritious** (adj): full of nutrients; healthy to eat
  - Usage: "I try to prepare nutritious meals with plenty of vegetables."
- **balanced diet** (noun phrase): a diet containing the right proportions of all food groups
  - Usage: "Cooking at home allows me to maintain a balanced diet."
- **home-cooked meal** (noun phrase): a meal prepared at home from scratch
  - Usage: "There's something satisfying about a home-cooked meal."
- **dining out** (phrasal noun): eating at restaurants
  - Usage: "Dining out frequently can be quite costly."
- **budget-friendly** (adj): inexpensive; good value for money
  - Usage: "Cooking at home is more budget-friendly than eating out."
- **meal prep** (noun): preparing meals in advance
  - Usage: "I do meal prep on Sundays to save time during the week."

#### Health & Nutrition
- **wholesome** (adj): good for health; nutritious
  - Usage: "I prefer wholesome ingredients over processed foods."
- **portion control** (noun phrase): managing the amount of food eaten
  - Usage: "Cooking at home gives me better portion control."
- **dietary habits** (noun phrase): the way a person typically eats
  - Usage: "My dietary habits improved significantly when I started cooking."

### Useful Linking Phrases
- "There's no denying that..."
- "From my perspective..."
- "The way I see it..."
- "One of the main reasons is that..."
- "Not to mention the fact that..."
- "What I particularly appreciate about... is..."

### Collocations to Remember
- **maintain** a balanced diet / a healthy lifestyle
- **prepare** a meal / home-cooked food
- **save** money / time / effort
- **cut down on** eating out / processed food / unhealthy snacks
- **have** control over ingredients / portions / what you eat
- **be worth** the effort / the time / it

### Sample Improved Response
> "Yes, I **tend to prefer** cooking at home because I like to **maintain a balanced diet**. **Not to mention the fact that** **dining out** can be quite **costly** and often **less nutritious** than what I'd prepare myself. **From my perspective**, cooking gives me **full control over** the ingredients and **portion sizes**, which is essential for a healthy lifestyle."
