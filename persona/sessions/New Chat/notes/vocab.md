### Professional Vocabulary — Sports / Basketball

- **avid** (adj.): very enthusiastic or dedicated
  - Usage: "I'm an avid basketball fan and I play regularly."
- **draft** (n.): the process by which NBA teams select new players
  - Usage: "Could you elaborate on how the NBA draft works?"
- **franchise** (n.): a professional sports team organization
  - Usage: "I'm curious about how different franchises approach the draft."
- **prospect** (n.): a potential player being evaluated for the draft
  - Usage: "I'd like to learn how scouts evaluate draft prospects."
- **roster** (n.): the list of players on a team
  - Usage: "The draft significantly impacts a team's roster."
- **playoffs** (n.): the post-season tournament
  - Usage: "The ultimate goal is to build a playoff-contending team."
- **standings** (n.): ranking of teams based on performance
  - Usage: "Teams with poorer standings get higher draft picks."
- **follow** (v.): to keep up with a team or league
  - Usage: "Do you follow the NBA regularly?"
- **all-star** (n./adj.): a top player selected for the annual showcase game
  - Usage: "He's a multiple-time all-star and one of the league's best."
- **championship** (n.): the title awarded to the league winner
  - Usage: "Every team's dream is to win the championship."
- **rivalry** (n.): intense competition between specific teams
  - Usage: "The Lakers-Celtics rivalry is legendary in NBA history."
- **MVP** (Most Valuable Player): the best player award
  - Usage: "Who do you think deserves the MVP this season?"

### Useful Linking Phrases

- "I'd like to know more about..."
- "Could you elaborate on..."
- "One aspect I'm particularly curious about is..."
- "To be honest, I'm not very well-versed in..."
- "From what I understand so far..."
- "That's a great question — let me think about it."
- **"I'd like to discuss..."** — more formal than "talk about"
- **"What's your take on...?"** — natural way to ask for opinions
- **"Speaking of which..."** — smooth topic transition
- **"When it comes to..."** — introducing a specific subtopic
- **"I'm particularly interested in..."** — showing focused interest

### Collocations to Remember

- **follow** + the NBA / a team / the draft / the league
- **be** + an avid fan / a casual viewer / a regular player / a supporter of
- **have** + a passion for / an interest in / a good understanding of
- **build** + a team / a roster / a championship contender
- **evaluate** + prospects / talent / player potential
- **enter** + the draft / the league / the playoffs
- **win** + a championship / an MVP award / a title
- **support** + a team / a player / a franchise
- **root for** + a team / a player (informal but natural)
- **keep up with** + the season / the standings / the latest news

### Sample Improved Response

> **Original:** "I'd like to talk something about NBA game. And do you like NBA? What's your favorite team, and who's your favorite player?"
