## Scoring Criteria

### Fluency & Coherence (FC)
- **Band 9**: Speaks fluently at length; natural hesitation only when reflecting on ideas; no effort to search for words; uses discourse markers and connectives seamlessly
- **Band 8**: Speaks at length with occasional hesitation; searches for words occasionally but with no blocking; uses discourse markers and connectors appropriately
- **Band 7**: Generally speaks fluently; may show hesitation when searching for ideas; uses some discourse markers and connectives; occasional repetition or self-correction
- **Band 6**: Can keep going but often hesitates; uses simple discourse markers; some repetition and self-correction; occasional long pauses
- **Band 5**: Frequent hesitation; often pauses mid-sentence; limited use of discourse markers; may repeat self or others

### Lexical Resource (LR)
- **Band 9**: Uses vocabulary with full flexibility and precision; paraphrases skillfully
- **Band 8**: Wide vocabulary range; occasional inappropriateness; can discuss abstract topics
- **Band 7**: Enough vocabulary to discuss topics at length; some inappropriateness; demonstrates usage of less common words
- **Band 6**: Enough vocabulary to convey general meaning; occasional word selection errors; shows ability to discuss familiar topics
- **Band 5**: Limited vocabulary requiring interpretation; frequent lexical errors; struggles with unfamiliar topics

### Grammatical Range & Accuracy (GRA)
- **Band 9**: Uses full range of structures flexibly and accurately; sophisticated control of complex structures
- **Band 8**: Wide range of structures; occasional errors in complex structures; meaningful communication
- **Band 7**: Variety of sentence structures; some complex sentences; errors don't impede communication
- **Band 6**: Mix of simple and complex sentences; some grammatical errors; meaning generally clear
- **Band 5**: Short simple sentences; limited variety; frequent grammatical errors

### Pronunciation (P)
- **Band 9**: Full range of features; easy to understand; L1 accent doesn't affect communication
- **Band 8**: All features used appropriately; generally easy to understand; L1 accent may be noticeable
- **Band 7**: Generally clear; some features may be inconsistent; L1 influence may be evident
- **Band 6**: Mostly clear despite some errors; some L1 influence; intonation patterns sometimes incorrect
- **Band 5**: Difficult to understand at times; frequent pronunciation errors; L1 influence is significant
